# Usage

Drop mod jar in mods folder, launch game! 

- Open this mod's config screen (whether via ModMenu or buttons in the multiplayer screen)
- In "General settings" section, turn on "Use proxy".
- In "Socks proxy chain" list, add a list of proxies you have one by one. 
  Choose SOCKS proxy version your proxy accepts. (SOCKS5 is highly recommended. SOCKS4 is pretty old.)
  Fill in host address, port number.
  If authentication is in demand, fill in username and password field. (SOCKS4 wants username field only)
- Hit save button. Done.
- After saving new config, it's better to restart your game.
  Alternatively, you can press "Test proxy" button to test your proxy chain's connectivity.

There are more settings in "Server settings" section. Adjust them accordingly to satisfy your need.
